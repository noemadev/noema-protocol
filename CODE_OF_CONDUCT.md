# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in the Noema Protocol community
a respectful and professional experience for everyone.

We are committed to fostering an open environment focused on
constructive discussion, technical rigor, and mutual respect.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Focusing on technical merit and system design
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Trolling or disruptive behavior
- Publishing private information without consent
- Any conduct that undermines professional collaboration

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including:

- GitHub repositories
- Issues and pull requests
- Discussions and documentation

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct.

They have the right and responsibility to remove, edit,
or reject contributions that do not align with these standards.

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior,
please report it privately to the maintainers listed
in `MAINTAINERS.md`.

All reports will be reviewed and handled discreetly.
