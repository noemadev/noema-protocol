# Maintainers

This document lists the current maintainers of Noema Protocol.

Maintainers are responsible for the direction, integrity,
and governance of the protocol.

---

## Current Maintainers

- **noemdev**  
  GitHub: https://github.com/noemdev

---

## Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for:

- Reviewing and merging contributions
- Approving or rejecting RFCs
- Managing releases and versioning
- Handling security reports
- Maintaining documentation quality

---

## Changes to Maintainers

Changes to the maintainer list are handled
by existing maintainers and documented publicly.

As the project evolves, additional maintainers
may be added based on sustained contributions
and alignment with project goals.
